# Rep 016 — Stock Up Dinners

**Status:** Building
**Website:** [stockupdinners.com](https://stockupdinners.com)

Costco-anchored dinner meal plan info product. Free 14-dinner lead magnet + $25 Gumroad PDF (6-week system). Target: 200 email signups in 30 days.

## Launch Checklist

Sequential. Each phase unlocks the next. Go as fast as you can.

### Phase 1 — Foundation (do first, everything depends on this)

- [x] **1. Build the master Costco staple ingredient list**
  DONE — 75 verified items across 7 categories. See [Costco Master Staple Ingredient List](#costco-master-staple-ingredient-list) below.

- [x] **2. AI-generate 14 dinners for the free bi-weekly plan**
  DONE — 14 meals across 2 cook days with full recipes, storage instructions, and consolidated buying list. See [14-Dinner Meal Prep Plan](#14-dinner-meal-prep-plan--costco-staples-only) below.

- [ ] **3. Vet every recipe against real Costco inventory**
  Check each ingredient on Costco.com. Flag anything seasonal, regional, or not actually available. Swap as needed. Verify cost estimates are in the right ballpark.

- [ ] **4. Build consolidated shopping lists (Week 1 + Week 2)**
  Aggregate all ingredients across 7 dinners per week. Deduplicate. Calculate quantities for a family of 4. Estimate total weekly grocery cost. Format as a printable checklist.

- [ ] **5. Organize into Week 1 and Week 2 with logical meal sequencing**
  No back-to-back same protein. Mix quick meals with longer cooks. Put easiest meals on Mon/Fri (tired nights). Weekend meals can be more involved.

### Phase 2 — Product (the PDF)

- [ ] **6. Create brand assets in Canva**
  Logo (simple wordmark is fine), color palette, fonts. Build a PDF template with cover page layout, recipe page layout, shopping list layout, and CTA page layout. These templates get reused for the paid product too.

- [ ] **7. Design and build the free PDF**
  Structure: cover -> how to use this plan (1 pg) -> Week 1 meal grid + shopping list -> Week 1 recipes -> Week 2 meal grid + shopping list -> Week 2 recipes -> CTA page ("The full 6-week system is coming"). Target: 20-30 pages. Must look professional and be printable.

### Phase 3 — Infrastructure (can run parallel with Phase 2)

- [ ] **8. Set up Beehiiv account + connect domain**
  Create account. Point stockupdinners.com DNS to Beehiiv. Pick a clean template. This can happen while the PDF is being designed.

- [ ] **9. Build the landing page**
  Headline + 3 bullet value props + email capture + PDF preview image. Keep it dead simple. One page, one action: enter your email, get the plan.

- [ ] **10. Write + set up welcome email sequence**
  Email 1 (instant): delivers PDF + quick start tips. Email 2 (day 3): "How's Week 1 going?" engagement check. Email 3 (day 7): teaser for the full 6-week system. Configure automation in Beehiiv.

- [ ] **11. Test the full funnel end-to-end**
  Sign up with a burner email. Confirm PDF arrives. Check formatting on mobile + desktop. Click every link. Fix anything broken.

### Phase 4 — Distribution (launch)

- [ ] **12. Create Pinterest business account + design 10-15 pins**
  Pin concepts: meal grid previews, shopping list teasers, "14 dinners from one warehouse trip" hooks, individual meal photos. Schedule across the first 2 weeks. All link to landing page.

- [ ] **13. Draft + post Reddit content**
  Value-first posts for r/Costco, r/MealPrepSunday, r/EatCheapAndHealthy. Share a sample week in the post body. Link to full PDF in comments only. Post Sunday afternoon for peak traffic. Engage every comment.

- [ ] **14. Join + post in Facebook Costco groups**
  Join 3-5 active groups now (approval delays). Post value-first content once approved. Same approach as Reddit — lead with the meals, link in comments.

- [ ] **15. Set up Gumroad for paid product**
  Create account. Build the $25 product listing page (even if the paid PDF isn't ready yet — use "coming soon" or waitlist). This lets Email 3 in the sequence link somewhere real.

### Phase 5 — Post-launch (after signups start)

- [ ] **16. Generate the 3 paid bi-weekly plans (42 more dinners)**
  Same process as the free plan. Some meal overlap with the free plan is fine if it keeps shopping lists efficient. Design into the same Canva template. Package as the $25 Gumroad PDF.

- [ ] **17. Monitor metrics + iterate distribution**
  Track: signups/day, email open rates, which channel drives traffic. Double down on what's working. Target: 200 signups in 30 days.

### Parallel tracks

Tasks 8-11 (infrastructure) can run alongside tasks 6-7 (PDF design). Task 14 (Facebook groups) — join groups immediately since approval takes time. Everything else is sequential.

### Success metrics at day 30

- 200 email signups

## 14-Dinner Meal Prep Plan — Costco Staples Only

**Format:** 2 big cook days -> 7 freezer-ready dinners each
**Serves:** 4 per meal (family-style serving)
**Storage:** All meals stored family-style in half-size aluminum foil pans (approx 12.75" x 10.25" x 2.5") with foil lids, gallon freezer bags, or a combination. No individual portioning.
**Freezer method:** Cool completely -> seal with foil lids or press air from bags -> freeze flat for stacking

### Storage Key

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| PAN | Half-size foil pan with foil lid -- reheat directly in oven |
| 2-PAN | Two foil pans (one for protein/sauce, one for starch or veg) |
| BAG | Gallon freezer bag, frozen flat |
| COMBO | Components stored separately -- bags for some, pan for others |

### OVEN REHEAT QUICK REFERENCE

| Temp | Time (covered) | Time (uncovered) | Use For |
|------|---------------|-----------------|---------|
| 325F | 15-18 min | -- | Fish (salmon, cod) -- low heat protects texture |
| 350F | 20-25 min | -- | Rice, quinoa, pasta, curries, delicate proteins (pork, shrimp) |
| 375F | 25-30 min | 10 min (cheese top) | Hearty mains: chili, Bolognese, tikka, burrito filling, baked penne, chicken parm |

#### Tips for Best Results
- **Rice pans:** Sprinkle 2-3 tbsp water over rice before covering. Steam rehydrates the grains.
- **Pasta pans:** Drizzle EVOO before freezing and add a splash of water before reheating.
- **Cheese-topped pans (Meals 7, 14):** Keep covered until last 10 min, then uncover for browning.
- **Coconut curry (Meal 6):** Stay at 350F -- coconut milk separates at higher heat. Stir gently.
- **Peanut noodles (Meal 11):** Always add water before reheating. The sauce thickens considerably when frozen.

### MEAL DETAILS -- ALL 12 DINNERS

#### Meal 15 -- Turkey & Garbanzo Chili

**Protein:** 2 lb ground turkey (remaining from Cycle 1)
**Pantry consumed:** 2 cans garbanzo beans, 2 cans diced tomatoes, 1 can tomato paste, 2 cans corn, onion, garlic, garlic powder, salt, pepper
**Method:** Brown turkey with diced onion and garlic. Add garbanzo beans (drained), diced tomatoes, tomato paste, corn (drained), garlic powder, salt, pepper. Simmer 30 min. Single-pot meal.
**Storage:** PAN. Top with cheddar at the table.
**Reheat:** 375F covered 30-35 min, stir halfway.

---

#### Meal 16 -- Chicken Parm Penne Bake

**Protein:** 2 lb Just Bare Chicken Chunks (remaining from Cycle 1)
**Pantry consumed:** 2 lb short-cut pasta, 2 cans tomato sauce, 16 oz mozzarella, 3 oz parm
**Method:** Cook penne. Bake or air-fry Just Bare chunks per package. Layer in foil pan: penne -> tomato sauce -> chicken chunks -> more sauce -> mozzarella and parm on top. Bake 375F 20 min if eating now, or assemble and freeze.
**Storage:** PAN, fully assembled casserole.
**Reheat:** 375F covered 30 min, uncovered 10 min until cheese bubbles.

---

#### Meal 17 -- Coconut Chicken & Garbanzo Curry

**Protein:** 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded (~1.5 lb meat)
**Pantry consumed:** 2 cans coconut milk, 2 cans garbanzo beans, 1 can tomato paste, 1 lb frozen broccoli, ~1.5 lb jasmine rice, onion, garlic, garlic powder, salt
**Method:** Saute onion and garlic in avocado oil. Add coconut milk, tomato paste, garlic powder, salt. Simmer 10 min. Stir in drained garbanzo beans and broccoli. Fold in shredded chicken. Serve over jasmine rice.
**Storage:** 2-PAN. Pan 1: curry. Pan 2: rice.
**Reheat:** Curry 350F 20-25 min. Rice 350F 20 min (sprinkle water first).

---

#### Meal 18 -- Chicken, Potato & Green Bean Plates

**Protein:** 1 rotisserie chicken, carved/pulled
**Pantry consumed:** 4 lb potatoes, 2 lb frozen green beans, 4 oz butter, garlic, salt, pepper
**Method:** Cube potatoes, toss with avocado oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder. Roast at 400F 30 min. Saute green beans in butter with minced garlic. Pull/carve chicken. Plate family-style: roasted potatoes, buttered green beans, chicken.
**Storage:** 2-PAN. Pan 1: chicken pieces. Pan 2: potatoes on one side, green beans on the other.
**Reheat:** Both pans 350F covered 20-25 min.

---

#### Meal 19 -- Chicken Tikka Pasta Bake

**Protein:** 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded
**Pantry consumed:** 2 lb short-cut pasta, 2 cans tomato sauce, 1 can tomato paste, 4 oz yogurt, 16 oz mozzarella, 3 oz parm, onion, garlic, garlic powder, salt
**Method:** Cook penne. Make tikka-style sauce: saute onion and garlic, add tomato sauce + tomato paste + garlic powder + salt, simmer 15 min, stir in yogurt. Toss shredded chicken into sauce. Layer in foil pan: pasta -> tikka chicken sauce -> mozzarella + parm. Bake 375F 20 min.
**Storage:** PAN, fully assembled.
**Reheat:** 375F covered 30 min, uncovered 10 min.

---

#### Meal 20 -- Chicken Quesadillas + Black Bean Corn Salad

**Protein:** 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded
**Pantry consumed:** 8 flour tortillas, 8 oz cheddar, 1 can black beans, 1 can corn, salt, pepper
**Method:** Mix shredded chicken with half the cheddar. Fill tortillas, press in a hot skillet until golden. Separately, toss drained black beans + drained corn with a splash of EVOO, salt, pepper for a cold side salad.
**Storage:** BAG. Gallon bag: assembled uncooked quesadillas stacked with parchment between them. Quart bag: bean-corn salad.
**Reheat:** Thaw. Cook quesadillas in a dry skillet 3 min per side. Serve bean salad cold.

---

#### Meal 21 -- Peanut Chicken Noodles v2

**Protein:** 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded
**Pantry consumed:** 1.5 lb spaghetti, ~8 oz peanut butter, soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, 1.5 lb frozen edamame, 1 lb frozen spinach
**Method:** Cook spaghetti. Make peanut sauce: whisk peanut butter + soy sauce + honey + garlic + warm water. Cook edamame. Thaw and squeeze spinach. Toss everything together: noodles + sauce + chicken + edamame + spinach.
**Storage:** PAN, fully combined. Drizzle extra sauce on top before freezing.
**Reheat:** 350F covered 25-30 min. Add 3-4 tbsp water before sealing -- sauce thickens when frozen.

---

#### Meal 22 -- Coconut Chicken & Broccoli Rice Bowls

**Protein:** 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded + any remaining chicken breast/tenderloin scraps (~1.5 lb combined)
**Pantry consumed:** 2 cans coconut milk, 2 lb frozen broccoli, ~1.5 lb jasmine rice, soy sauce, garlic, salt
**Method:** Saute garlic in avocado oil, add coconut milk and a splash of soy sauce, simmer 10 min. Steam broccoli. Fold chicken into coconut sauce. Serve over rice with broccoli on the side.
**Storage:** 2-PAN. Pan 1: chicken in coconut sauce + broccoli. Pan 2: rice.
**Reheat:** Both pans 350F covered 20 min. Stir curry gently.

---

#### Meal 23 -- Chicken & Garbanzo Quinoa Bowls

**Protein:** 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded
**Pantry consumed:** 2 cans garbanzo beans, 1.5 lb quinoa, ~4 oz yogurt, EVOO, balsamic vinegar, salt, pepper
**Method:** Cook quinoa. Warm garbanzo beans. Shred chicken. Make dressing: EVOO + balsamic + salt + pepper. Bowl assembly: quinoa -> chicken -> garbanzo -> drizzle of yogurt -> balsamic vinaigrette -> fresh spinach on top.
**Storage:** 2-PAN. Pan 1: chicken + garbanzo (layered, not mixed). Pan 2: quinoa. Jar: vinaigrette (fridge). Yogurt dolloped day-of.
**Reheat:** Quinoa 350F 20 min. Chicken-garbanzo pan 350F 20 min. Add fresh spinach and dressings at the table.

---

#### Meal 24 -- Loaded Baked Potato Bar

**Protein:** 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded
**Pantry consumed:** 4 lb potatoes, 8 oz cheddar, 1 can black beans, 1 can corn, butter, salt, pepper
**Method:** Bake potatoes whole at 400F for 50-60 min (or cube and roast 30 min for speed). Warm black beans and corn separately. Shred chicken. Set out all toppings bar-style: chicken, beans, corn, shredded cheddar, butter, sour cream (if bought). Everyone builds their own.
**Storage:** PAN: shredded chicken + black beans + corn combined. Potatoes baked fresh day-of (they don't freeze well whole). Or cube and roast, then freeze in a separate pan.
**Reheat:** Topping pan 375F 20 min. Bake potatoes fresh, or reheat cubed potatoes 375F 25 min.

---

#### Meal 25 -- Chicken Marinara Wraps

**Protein:** 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded
**Pantry consumed:** 8 flour tortillas, 2 cans tomato sauce, 8 oz mozzarella, 1 can black beans, garlic, salt, pepper
**Method:** Simmer tomato sauce with garlic, salt, pepper for 10 min. Toss in drained black beans and shredded chicken. Warm tortillas. Spoon chicken-marinara-bean mix into tortillas, top with mozzarella. Can eat as wraps or bake seam-side down at 375F 15 min for a crispy finish.
**Storage:** BAG: chicken-marinara-bean filling frozen flat. Tortillas and mozzarella stored separately.
**Reheat:** Thaw filling. Warm in a pan or microwave. Assemble wraps and optionally bake 15 min for crispiness.

---

#### Meal 26 -- Chicken & Corn Chowder

**Protein:** 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded + 1 lb remaining cod (optional)
**Pantry consumed:** 2 lb potatoes, 2 cans corn, 1 can diced tomatoes, 4 oz butter, onion, garlic, salt, pepper
**Method:** Dice potatoes small. Saute onion in butter, add garlic, then potatoes, corn (drained), diced tomatoes, and 4-5 cups water (or chicken stock if you have it from the master list). Simmer 20-25 min until potatoes are tender. Stir in shredded chicken (and flaked cod if using). Season well. For creaminess, mash some potatoes against the side of the pot.
**Storage:** PAN -- this is a hearty one-pot soup.
**Reheat:** 375F covered 30-35 min, stir halfway. Add a splash of water if too thick.

### COOK DAY 3 -- MINI BATCH WORKFLOW

**Prep window:** ~2 hours | **Yields:** Meals 15-16 plus prep-ahead for 17-18

#### Phase 1: Ovens and Pots (0:00)

**Turkey Chili (Meal 15):** Brown 2 lb ground turkey with diced onion and garlic. Add 2 cans garbanzo (drained), 2 cans diced tomatoes, 1 can tomato paste, 2 cans corn (drained), garlic powder, salt, pepper. Simmer 30 min.

**Short-Cut Pasta:** Boil 4 lb short-cut pasta. This covers both Meal 16 (2 lb) and Meal 19 (2 lb). Drizzle with EVOO to prevent clumping.

**Just Bare Chunks (Meal 16):** Bake or air-fry 2 lb per package directions.

#### Phase 2: Assembly (1:00)

**Meal 15 -- Turkey Garbanzo Chili:** Ladle into foil pan. Done.

**Meal 16 -- Chicken Parm Penne Bake:** Layer in foil pan: 2 lb cooked penne -> 2 cans tomato sauce -> chicken chunks -> mozzarella + parm on top.

**Meal 19 -- Tikka Pasta Bake (prep-ahead):** Bag the remaining 2 lb pasta in a gallon freezer bag. On the day you cook Meal 19, you'll only need to make the tikka sauce and assemble.

#### Phase 3: Cool, Label, Freeze (1:30-2:00)

Cool both pans. Seal with foil lids. Label: meal name + date + reheat temp. Freeze.
