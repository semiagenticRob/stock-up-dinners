## How to Post (Without Getting Banned)

Most Costco Facebook groups have anti-spam rules. The key is value-first posting.

**DO:**
- Share the actual meal plan content (the recipes, the shopping list, the weekly sequence)
- Say "I made a free guide — link in comments if you want it" rather than leading with the link
- Engage authentically — respond to every comment
- Post on Sunday afternoon / Monday morning (peak engagement for meal planning content)

**DON'T:**
- Post a link in the post body (gets flagged as spam/affiliate)
- Use promotional language ("buy," "sign up," "free download") in the post title
- Post the same content to multiple groups on the same day

**The post format that works:**
```
We eat out of Costco for ~$43 per family dinner — 14 different meals across 2 weeks from one big shop. No repeated proteins back-to-back, no processed shortcuts.

Week 1: Coconut Shrimp Curry, Chicken Burrito Bowls, Beef Bolognese, Teriyaki Salmon, Chicken Stir-Fry, Turkey Chili, Baked Penne
Week 2: Beef & Black Bean Burritos, Chicken Tikka Bowls, Salmon Quinoa Bowls, Peanut Chicken Noodles, Fish Taco Kits, Roasted Pork Tenderloin, Chicken Parm & Pasta

The shopping list + full meal plan is free if anyone wants it — drop a 🛒 in the comments and I'll link you.
```

This format works because: it leads with value (14 meals), gives a real preview (the week sequences), and uses engagement mechanics (emoji comment CTA) that keep the post visible in the algorithm.
