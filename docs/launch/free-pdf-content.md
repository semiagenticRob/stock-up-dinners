## Page 2: How to Use This Plan

### One Big Shop. Two Cook Days. Zero Stress.

Here's how the system works:

**Week 1 — The Big Shop (~$555–575)**
One trip to Costco covers all proteins, canned goods, grains, dairy, and frozen veg for both weeks. You'll spend about 2 hours cooking on Saturday, then 1.5 hours on Sunday. Dinner is ready every night in 20 minutes or less.

**Week 2 — The Top-Up (~$50–60)**
A quick mid-week grocery run for fresh proteins (pork tenderloin, tilapia, chicken chunks, cabbage). Everything else is already in your fridge and pantry.

**Combined cost: ~$595–635 total**
That's roughly $43–45 per family dinner for a family of four. Under $11 per person, per meal.

**Two cook days per week:**
- **Saturday afternoon** — 2 hours: the major cook day
- **Sunday afternoon** — 1.5 hours: finishing the week

You'll see which meals come from each cook day throughout this guide.

### Meal 1 — Coconut Shrimp Curry with Jasmine Rice
*Monday · Serves 4–6 · Cook Day: Saturday*

**Ingredients**
- 1 lb Kirkland Raw Shrimp (thawed)
- 2 cans Kirkland Coconut Milk
- 3 tbsp yellow curry paste (or Trader Joe's Thai Yellow Curry Sauce)
- 1 cup Kirkland Normandy Blend Veg (thawed)
- 2 cups jasmine rice (cooked)
- Salt, garlic, olive oil

**Cook Day Prep (Saturday)**
1. Thaw shrimp in cold water, 20 min. Pat dry.
2. Sauté garlic in olive oil, 2 min.
3. Add curry paste, stir 1 min. Pour in coconut milk.
4. Simmer 5 min, add veg, cook 5 min more.
5. Add shrimp last — 3 min until pink. Do not overcook.
6. Cool completely. Store in covered container in fridge.

**Monday Night (20 min)**
1. Reheat curry in saucepan on medium-low, covered, 10 min.
2. Microwave rice 3 min.
3. Serve curry over rice.

---

### Meal 2 — Chicken Burrito Bowls
*Tuesday · Serves 4–6 · Cook Day: Saturday*

**Ingredients**
- 1.5 lb Just Bare Chicken Chunks
- 1 can Bush's Black Beans (drained, rinsed)
- 1 cup jasmine rice (cooked)
- 1 can diced tomatoes
- Kirkland Shredded Mexican Cheese Blend
- Kirkland Chicken Seasoning, olive oil, garlic

**Cook Day Prep (Saturday)**
1. Season chicken chunks with chicken seasoning. Sear in olive oil, 6–8 min total until cooked through. Rest 5 min, then slice.
2. Warm beans in small pot with garlic, pinch of salt, 5 min.
3. Combine chicken, beans, diced tomatoes. Cool and refrigerate.

**Tuesday Night (20 min)**
1. Reheat chicken-bean mixture in pan, 8–10 min.
2. Microwave rice 3 min.
3. Layer: rice → chicken-bean mix → cheese. Optional: sour cream, hot sauce.

---

### Meal 3 — Beef Bolognese over Penne
*Wednesday · Serves 4–6 · Cook Day: Saturday*

**Ingredients**
- 1.5 lb Ground Beef
- 1 jar Kirkland Marinara Sauce (25 oz)
- 1 can diced tomatoes
- Penne (cook 12 oz Wednesday night)
- Parmesan, garlic, olive oil, Italian seasoning

**Cook Day Prep (Saturday)**
1. Brown ground beef with garlic, drain fat.
2. Add marinara + diced tomatoes. Simmer 15 min. Season to taste.
3. Cool completely. Refrigerate in covered container.

**Wednesday Night (25 min)**
1. Boil water, cook penne 10 min.
2. Reheat bolognese in saucepan on medium, 8–10 min.
3. Serve sauce over pasta with parmesan.

### Meal 4 — Teriyaki Salmon with Stir-Fry Veg
*Thursday · Serves 4–6 · Cook Day: Sunday*

**Ingredients**
- 1 lb Kirkland Salmon Fillets
- 3 tbsp Kirkland Teriyaki Sauce
- 2 cups Kirkland Stir-Fry Veg (frozen)
- 1 cup jasmine rice
- Sesame oil (optional), garlic, olive oil

**Cook Day Prep (Sunday)**
1. Brush salmon with teriyaki sauce. Bake at 400°F for 12–14 min. Cool and refrigerate whole fillets.
2. Stir-fry vegetables in olive oil + garlic 5 min. Season. Cool and refrigerate.
3. Cook rice. Store.

**Thursday Night (20 min)**
1. Reheat salmon uncovered in 350°F oven, 8 min.
2. Microwave veg 2 min, drizzle teriyaki.
3. Microwave rice 3 min.

---

### Meal 5 — Chicken Stir-Fry with Rice
*Friday · Serves 4–6 · Cook Day: Saturday*

**Ingredients**
- 1 lb Just Bare Chicken Chunks
- 2 cups Kirkland Stir-Fry Veg
- 3 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)
- 1 tbsp minced garlic
- Jasmine rice

**Cook Day Prep (Saturday)**
1. Sauté garlic in oil, 1 min.
2. Add chicken chunks, cook 6–8 min until done.
3. Add stir-fry veg, soy sauce. Toss on high heat 3–4 min.
4. Cool and refrigerate.

**Friday Night (20 min)**
1. Reheat stir-fry in wok or large pan, 5–7 min on medium-high.
2. Microwave rice 3 min.

---

### Meal 6 — Turkey Chili
*Saturday · Serves 6–8 · Cook Day: Sunday*

**Ingredients**
- 1.5 lb Kirkland Ground Turkey
- 1 can Black Beans (drained)
- 1 can Diced Tomatoes
- 1 cup Chicken Broth
- Chili powder (2 tsp), cumin (1 tsp), garlic, olive oil
- Optional: Shredded cheese, sour cream

**Cook Day Prep (Sunday)**
1. Brown turkey with garlic and olive oil, 5–7 min.
2. Add all remaining ingredients. Simmer 20 min. Season to taste.
3. Cool completely. Refrigerate.

**Saturday Dinner (30 min)**
1. Reheat chili on stove, covered, 15 min.
2. Top with cheese and sour cream.

---

### Meal 7 — Baked Penne with Marinara
*Sunday · Serves 4–6 · Cook Day: Sunday*

**Ingredients**
- 12 oz penne
- 1 jar Kirkland Marinara Sauce
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella (or Mexican blend)
- Parmesan, olive oil

**Cook Day Prep (Sunday)**
1. Cook penne al dente (8 min), drain.
2. Toss with marinara. Transfer to baking dish.
3. Top with cheese + parmesan.
4. Cool and cover. Refrigerate unbaked.

**Sunday Dinner (30 min)**
1. Bake at 375°F, uncovered, 20–25 min until cheese is bubbly.

### Meal 8 — Beef & Black Bean Burritos
*Monday · Serves 4–6 · Cook Day: Saturday*

**Ingredients**
- 1 lb Ground Beef (remaining)
- 1 can Black Beans (drained)
- 1 can Diced Tomatoes
- Flour tortillas (buy if not stocked)
- Shredded cheese, sour cream

**Cook Day Prep (Saturday)**
1. Brown beef with garlic, drain fat.
2. Add black beans + drained diced tomatoes, simmer 8 min. Season.
3. Cool and refrigerate filling.

**Monday Night (20 min)**
1. Reheat filling in pan, 5 min.
2. Warm tortillas in microwave (30 sec wrapped in damp paper towel).
3. Assemble with cheese and sour cream.

---

### Meal 9 — Chicken Tikka-Style Bowls
*Tuesday · Serves 4–6 · Cook Day: Saturday*

**Ingredients**
- 1 lb Just Bare Chicken Chunks
- 1 can Diced Tomatoes
- 3 tbsp Greek Yogurt (Kirkland 48 oz tub)
- 2 tsp garam masala or chicken tikka seasoning, garlic, olive oil
- Jasmine rice

**Cook Day Prep (Saturday)**
1. Season chicken with garam masala. Sear in oil 6–8 min.
2. Add diced tomatoes, garlic, yogurt. Stir well. Simmer 8 min.
3. Cool and refrigerate.

**Tuesday Night (25 min)**
1. Reheat tikka on medium, covered, 10 min.
2. Microwave rice 3 min. Serve with fresh cilantro if available.

---

### Meal 10 — Salmon Quinoa Bowls
*Wednesday · Serves 4–6 · Cook Day: Saturday*

**Ingredients**
- 1 lb Kirkland Salmon Fillets
- 1 cup quinoa (dry → ~2 cups cooked)
- 1 cup Normandy Blend Veg (thawed)
- Lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, salt

**Cook Day Prep (Saturday)**
1. Cook quinoa (1:2 ratio, 15 min). Season with olive oil, lemon, salt.
2. Bake salmon at 400°F for 12–14 min. Cool, refrigerate.
3. Toss veg with oil and salt. Roast at 400°F for 15 min. Cool.

**Wednesday Night (20 min)**
1. Microwave quinoa 2 min.
2. Microwave veg 2 min.
3. Reheat salmon in oven at 350°F, 8 min. Flake over bowl.

---

### Meal 11 — Peanut Chicken Noodles
*Thursday · Serves 4–6 · Cook Day: Sunday*

**Ingredients**
- 1 lb Just Bare Chicken Chunks
- Annie Chun's Udon Noodles (2 packs)
- 3 tbsp peanut butter, 3 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp sesame oil (optional), garlic, olive oil
- Shredded cabbage (from Week 2 top-up)

**Cook Day Prep (Sunday)**
1. Cook chicken in oil, 6–8 min. Set aside.
2. Whisk peanut butter + soy sauce + honey + ¼ cup warm water until smooth.
3. Cook udon per package (3 min). Drain.
4. Toss noodles with sauce + chicken. Refrigerate in covered container.

**Thursday Night (20 min)**
1. Reheat noodles in pan with a splash of water, medium heat, 5–7 min.
2. Top with raw shredded cabbage.

---

### Meal 12 — Fish Taco Kits
*Friday · Serves 4–6 · Cook Day: Sunday*

**Ingredients**
- 1 lb Kirkland Tilapia Loins (thawed)
- Corn or flour tortillas
- Shredded cabbage
- Kirkland Greek Yogurt (as sour cream sub)
- Cumin (1 tsp), garlic powder, olive oil, lime juice

**Cook Day Prep (Sunday)**
1. Season tilapia with cumin + garlic powder. Pan-fry in olive oil 3–4 min per side.
2. Flake into large pieces. Cool and refrigerate.
3. Shred cabbage. Refrigerate separately.

**Friday Night (25 min)**
1. Reheat fish in pan, 4 min.
2. Warm tortillas 30 sec microwave.
3. Assemble: tortilla → fish → cabbage → yogurt drizzle → lime squeeze.

---

### Meal 13 — Roasted Pork Tenderloin
*Saturday · Serves 4–6 · Cook Day: Sunday*

**Ingredients**
- 1.5 lb Pork Tenderloin
- 2 tbsp Kirkland Teriyaki Sauce or Montreal seasoning
- Normandy Blend Veg (remaining)
- Olive oil, garlic

**Cook Day Prep (Sunday)**
1. Marinate pork in teriyaki or seasoning, 30 min minimum.
2. Sear in oven-safe pan on stovetop, all sides, 5 min.
3. Finish in oven at 425°F for 18–22 min (internal temp 145°F). Rest 5 min, slice.
4. Roast veg alongside. Cool everything, refrigerate.

**Saturday Dinner (30 min)**
1. Reheat pork slices in covered pan with splash of broth, 10 min on medium.
2. Microwave veg 3 min.

---

### Meal 14 — Chicken Parmesan & Pasta
*Sunday · Serves 4–6 · Cook Day: Sunday*

**Ingredients**
- 1.5 lb Just Bare Chicken Chunks (or slice remaining)
- 1 jar Kirkland Marinara Sauce
- Penne (12 oz)
- Shredded mozzarella + Parmesan
- Olive oil, garlic, Italian seasoning

**Cook Day Prep (Sunday)**
1. Pound chicken slightly flat (or use as-is). Season, sear 5–6 min per side.
2. Transfer to baking dish. Top with marinara + cheese. Bake 375°F, 20 min.
3. Cool and refrigerate.

**Sunday Dinner (35 min)**
1. Reheat chicken parm in 350°F oven, 15 min.
2. Cook pasta fresh (10 min). Toss with reserved marinara.
3. Serve chicken over pasta.
