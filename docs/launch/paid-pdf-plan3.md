## Week 5 Recipes

### Meal 1 — Smoked Gouda Chicken Pasta
*Costco items: Kirkland rotisserie chicken, Kirkland pasta, Kirkland smoked Gouda*

**Ingredients (serves 4-5)**
- 2 cups rotisserie chicken, shredded
- 12 oz penne pasta
- 1 cup smoked Gouda, shredded
- 1 cup half & half
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp butter
- Salt, pepper, Italian seasoning

**Instructions**
1. Cook pasta per package directions. Reserve ½ cup pasta water.
2. Sauté garlic in butter 1 min. Add half & half, bring to gentle simmer.
3. Add Gouda, stir until melted and smooth. Add pasta water if too thick.
4. Toss pasta and chicken in sauce. Season generously. Serve immediately.

**Cook Day Tip:** Shred full rotisserie chicken Sunday — use half here, half in Meal 4.

### Meal 2 — Teriyaki Salmon Rice Bowls
*Costco items: Kirkland sockeye salmon fillets, Kirkland jasmine rice, Kirkland teriyaki sauce*

**Ingredients (serves 4)**
- 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
- 3 cups jasmine rice
- ½ cup Kirkland teriyaki sauce
- 1 bag Kirkland Asian stir-fry vegetables
- Sesame seeds, green onion for garnish

**Instructions**
1. Marinate salmon in teriyaki sauce 15 min (or overnight).
2. Cook rice. Pan-sear or broil salmon 4 min per side, basting with marinade.
3. Stir-fry vegetables 5 min in sesame oil.
4. Bowl: rice → vegetables → salmon → extra teriyaki drizzle → garnish.

### Meal 3 — Cuban Black Bean Bowls
*Costco items: Kirkland black beans (canned), Kirkland chicken thighs, Kirkland jasmine rice*

**Ingredients (serves 5)**
- 6 bone-in chicken thighs
- 2 cans black beans, drained
- 2 cups rice
- 1 tbsp cumin, 1 tsp oregano, 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 orange (juice), 4 garlic cloves
- Lime, fresh cilantro, sour cream

**Instructions**
1. Season chicken with cumin, oregano, paprika, salt. Bake 400°F for 35 min.
2. Simmer black beans with garlic, cumin, orange juice 15 min until thickened.
3. Shred chicken off bone. Serve over rice with beans, lime crema (sour cream + lime), cilantro.

### Meal 4 — Pesto Chicken Flatbreads
*Costco items: Kirkland pesto, Kirkland naan flatbreads (bakery), rotisserie chicken (leftover)*

**Ingredients (serves 4)**
- 4 naan flatbreads
- ½ cup Kirkland basil pesto
- 1 cup rotisserie chicken, shredded (leftover from Meal 1)
- 1 cup mozzarella, shredded
- ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- Fresh basil

**Instructions**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Spread pesto on flatbreads. Top with chicken, mozzarella, tomatoes.
3. Bake 10-12 min until cheese bubbles and edges crisp.
4. Finish with fresh basil. Cut into pieces.

**Cook Day Tip:** These go from fridge to table in 15 minutes — perfect for busy Friday nights.

---

### Meal 5 — Garlic Butter Shrimp Linguine
*Costco items: Kirkland raw shrimp (2 lb bag), Kirkland linguine, Kirkland unsalted butter*

**Ingredients (serves 4-5)**
- 1 lb raw shrimp, thawed and peeled
- 12 oz linguine
- 4 tbsp butter
- 6 garlic cloves, minced
- ½ cup white wine or chicken broth
- Lemon juice, red pepper flakes, fresh parsley

**Instructions**
1. Cook linguine al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
2. Sauté garlic in butter 1 min. Add shrimp, cook 2 min per side until pink.
3. Add wine/broth, lemon juice, pepper flakes. Simmer 2 min.
4. Toss pasta in sauce, adding pasta water as needed. Top with parsley.

### Meal 6 — Slow Cooker Beef Barbacoa Tacos
*Costco items: Kirkland beef chuck roast, Kirkland tortillas, Kirkland salsa verde*

**Ingredients (serves 6-8)**
- 3 lb beef chuck roast
- 1 can chipotle peppers in adobo (use 2-3 peppers)
- 4 garlic cloves, 1 tbsp cumin, 1 tbsp oregano
- ½ cup beef broth
- Juice of 2 limes
- Street taco toppings: onion, cilantro, salsa verde, lime

**Instructions**
1. Blend chipotle, garlic, cumin, oregano, broth, lime juice. Pour over beef in slow cooker.
2. Cook LOW 7-8 hours. Shred with forks. Toss in juices.
3. Serve in warm tortillas with all the toppings.

**Cook Day Tip:** Set this Sunday morning. Done by dinner. Leftover barbacoa makes excellent burrito bowls the next day.

### Meal 7 — Roasted Lemon Herb Chicken Thighs + Veggies
*Costco items: Kirkland chicken thighs, Kirkland baby potatoes, Kirkland broccoli florets*

**Ingredients (serves 5)**
- 6 bone-in chicken thighs
- 1.5 lb baby potatoes, halved
- 3 cups broccoli florets
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon (zest + juice), 4 garlic cloves, fresh rosemary

**Instructions**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Toss potatoes with oil, salt, pepper. Roast 15 min.
2. Season chicken with lemon zest, garlic, rosemary, salt. Nestle over potatoes.
3. Roast 25 more min. Add broccoli last 10 min.
4. Squeeze lemon over everything before serving.

## Week 6 Recipes

### Meal 8 — Butter Chicken with Basmati Rice
*Costco items: Kirkland chicken thighs (boneless), Kirkland basmati rice, Kirkland heavy cream*

**Ingredients (serves 5)**
- 2 lbs boneless chicken thighs, cubed
- 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 3 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp garam masala, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp ginger
- 4 garlic cloves, 1 onion, diced
- 3 cups basmati rice

**Instructions**
1. Cook rice. Sauté onion in butter 5 min. Add garlic, ginger, spices — 1 min.
2. Add chicken, brown 5 min. Add crushed tomatoes, simmer 15 min.
3. Stir in heavy cream, simmer 5 more min. Season.
4. Serve over rice. Naan if you have it.

---

### Meal 9 — Maple Dijon Salmon + Roasted Asparagus
*Costost items: Kirkland salmon, Kirkland asparagus (fresh or frozen)*

**Ingredients (serves 4)**
- 4 salmon fillets
- 3 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
- Salt, pepper, lemon

**Instructions**
1. Mix mustard + maple syrup. Brush generously on salmon.
2. Toss asparagus with olive oil, salt, pepper. Arrange on sheet pan.
3. Place salmon alongside asparagus. Bake 400°F for 15-18 min.
4. Squeeze lemon over everything. Done.

---

### Meal 10 — Turkey & Sweet Potato Stuffed Peppers
*Costco items: Kirkland ground turkey, Kirkland bell peppers, Kirkland sweet potatoes*

**Ingredients (serves 5)**
- 5 large bell peppers, halved and seeded
- 1.5 lbs ground turkey
- 2 medium sweet potatoes, small dice
- 1 can diced tomatoes, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 cup shredded cheese
- Fresh cilantro

**Instructions**
1. Roast sweet potatoes at 400°F 20 min. Cook turkey until browned; drain.
2. Mix turkey, sweet potato, tomatoes, cumin, chili powder. Season.
3. Fill pepper halves, top with cheese. Bake at 375°F 25 min until peppers tender.

---

### Meal 11 — Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken
*Costco items: Kirkland boneless chicken breasts, Kirkland sun-dried tomatoes in oil, Kirkland heavy cream*

**Ingredients (serves 4)**
- 4 chicken breasts
- ⅓ cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
- 1 cup heavy cream
- ½ cup chicken broth
- 4 garlic cloves, 2 tbsp fresh basil
- Pasta or crusty bread for serving

**Instructions**
1. Season and sear chicken 5-6 min per side in skillet. Remove.
2. Sauté garlic in same pan. Add sun-dried tomatoes, broth, cream. Simmer 3 min.
3. Return chicken to pan, simmer 5 more min. Top with basil.
4. Serve over pasta or with bread to soak up the sauce.

---

### Meal 12 — Fish Tacos with Avocado Crema
*Costco items: Kirkland tilapia fillets, Kirkland tortillas, Kirkland avocados (or guacamole)*

**Ingredients (serves 4)**
- 4 tilapia fillets
- 1 tbsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, ½ tsp garlic powder
- 1 ripe avocado, ½ cup sour cream, lime juice (crema)
- Kirkland coleslaw mix
- Corn tortillas
- Lime, hot sauce

**Instructions**
1. Season tilapia with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt. Pan-sear 3 min per side.
2. Blend avocado + sour cream + lime juice + salt until smooth.
3. Warm tortillas. Top with flaked fish, coleslaw, avocado crema, hot sauce.

---

### Meal 13 — Sheet Pan Sausage + Peppers + Potatoes
*Costco items: Kirkland Italian sausage (links), Kirkland bell peppers, Kirkland baby potatoes*

**Ingredients (serves 5)**
- 6 Italian sausage links, sliced into rounds
- 3 bell peppers, chunked
- 1.5 lb baby potatoes, quartered
- 1 red onion, chunked
- Olive oil, Italian seasoning, garlic powder

**Instructions**
1. Toss everything with olive oil, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt.
2. Spread on large sheet pan — single layer.
3. Roast 425°F for 35-40 min, tossing once halfway, until sausage crisps and potatoes brown.

**Cook Day Tip:** Zero cleanup. One pan in, one pan out. Perfect for the weekend.

---

### Meal 14 — Herb-Crusted Pork Tenderloin + Roasted Carrots
*Costco items: Kirkland pork tenderloin, Kirkland baby carrots*

**Ingredients (serves 5)**
- 2 pork tenderloins (~2 lbs total)
- 3 tbsp Dijon mustard
- Fresh rosemary, thyme, garlic — chopped fine
- 1 lb baby carrots
- Olive oil, honey, salt, pepper

**Instructions**
1. Brush tenderloins with mustard, press herb-garlic mixture over all sides.
2. Toss carrots with olive oil, honey, salt.
3. Sear tenderloins 2 min per side. Transfer to 400°F oven with carrots.
4. Roast 20-22 min until pork reaches 145°F. Rest 5 min, slice.
