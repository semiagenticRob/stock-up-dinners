# Stock Up Dinners — Beehiiv Setup Guide (Task 8 + Task 9)

CEO Agent overnight run, 2026-04-20.

Step-by-step guide to get stockupdinners.com live on Beehiiv before the April 21 deadline. Estimated time: 45–60 minutes. All copy is pre-written — you're just configuring and pasting.

## Pre-requisites (confirm before starting)

- [ ] You have the landing page copy from [[Stock Up Dinners - Landing Page Copy]]

## Step 1 — Create Beehiiv Account (5 min)

1. Go to **beehiiv.com** → click "Get started free"
2. Sign up with your email
3. Publication name: **Stock Up Dinners**
4. Description: "Free 14-dinner meal plan built from permanent Costco staples"
5. Choose the **Grow** plan (free tier is fine for launch — upgrade later if needed)
6. Skip the "import subscribers" step for now

---

## Step 2 — Connect Your Domain (10 min)

1. In Beehiiv dashboard → **Settings** → **Domain**
2. Click "Add custom domain"
3. Enter: `stockupdinners.com`
4. Beehiiv will show you DNS records to add (usually a CNAME pointing to `beehiiv.com`)
5. Log in to your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.)
6. Add the DNS record Beehiiv specifies
7. Click "Verify" in Beehiiv — propagation can take 5–30 minutes

> **Note:** The current `stockupdinners.com` redirects to `/lander` which 403s. Once you connect Beehiiv, the domain will point to your Beehiiv landing page instead.

## Step 5 — If PDF Isn't Ready Yet (launch workaround)

If the Canva PDF (Task 6/7) isn't finished in time, launch anyway with this workaround:

1. Instead of attaching the PDF in Welcome Email 1, say:
   > "Your plan is being finalized — I'll send it within 24 hours. (We're hand-reviewing every signup to make sure the plan lands correctly.)"

2. Manually compile the plan content from PLAN.md and email it as a nicely formatted HTML email or Google Doc link for first subscribers.

3. This "concierge launch" approach can actually increase trust with early adopters.
